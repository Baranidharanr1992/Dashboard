Production Optimization Networks

Production Optimization Leaders Network (POLN)

" POLN will develop, accelerate and sustain a strong culture of production optimization throughout the Upstream, and deliver strategic improvements in People, Practices and Technology. "

2021 POLN GOALS

Share Knowledge:

Activate and promote a culture of natural and agile knowledge sharing through networks, SharePoint, Yammer

Assess PO Health and improve visibility:

Perform asset maturity assessment, establish PO effectivness and identify improvement opportunities

Improve PO practitioner capability:

Elevate asset focus on PO practitioners, clarify PE roles & develop capabilities, inspire/empower operators to engage in PO

Through the POLN, production optimization strategies are connected from production managers to asset teams, and to working level practitioners. The POLN meet bi-monthly. Where are we in the world? View a map of the production managers network.

Asset Optimization Group (AOG)

" To help business units achieve production optimization goals through integrated engagement "

Mission Statement, est. 2020

There are total of (4) AOGs were formed based on asset type: Offshore, Onshore, FPSO, and Gas. They exist as working level focus groups with asset to asset collaboration on common problems. Monthly group meetings facilitated in partnership by the production engineering specialists (PES's) and AOG leads.
