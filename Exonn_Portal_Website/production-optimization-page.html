Production Optimization Page

What is Production Optimization?

Production Optimization Vision:

One team unlocking maximum value from producing assets by designing for success then relentlessly challenging and removing constraints from reservoir to sales point, every day

Why Production Optimization?

To maximize asset cash flow by extracting the most value from the reservoirs and process facilities

Our ability to optimize base production is directly dependant on:

  • The SAFETY of our operations,
  • RELIABILITY and INTEGRITY of our wells and equipment,
  • application of our TECHNICAL skills in Reservoir, Wells and Facilities, and
  • effective OPERATIONS and MAINTENANCE of our assets

How do we define "production optimization"?

Production Optimization is maximizing asset profitability by continously producing maximum economic volumes within the systems constrained capacities, while pursuing opportunities to increase capacity by relaxing or removing the constraints.

What does "production optimization"?

All members of the Asset Team have a role in production optimization across the entire asset production system: Production Engineers, Reservoir, FA, Wells, Facilities, Geoscience, Operations, Maintenance, Commercial, Regulatory...

How do Asset Level Teams optimize production?

Fundamentally, following the PO process map and improvement process where a multifunctional team looks at every point across the asset production system.
